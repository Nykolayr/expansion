Account and data deletion

App Expansion · com.ryjovs.expansion · operator: Danila Games

How to delete your account and related data in Expansion. Privacy policy: privacy-en.html.

1. Delete account in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Expansion on your device.
  2. Sign in with your email and password if needed.
  3. Open Profile.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm in the dialog.

Server-side account data is removed permanently and cannot be restored.

2. If you cannot access the app

Email autogid70@gmail.com with subject “Expansion account deletion” and include:

  • the email used for registration;
  • your nickname (if known);
  • a request to delete the account and all related data.

We will process the request within 30 days and reply to the same email.

3. Data that is deleted

  • email, nickname, optional display name;
  • password hash and sign-in tokens;
  • campaign progress, scores, meta upgrades;
  • leaderboard entry (if any);
  • donation history and ad-removal status tied to the account.

4. Data that may remain

  • On-device guest data (progress, settings, SQLite cache) until you uninstall the app or clear app storage in Android settings.
  • Support messages may remain in the developer mailbox for up to 12 months — you can ask to delete them by email.
  • Server security logs (IP, user-agent) up to 90 days, no longer linked to game progress after account deletion.
  • Yandex Mobile Ads may process device identifiers per Yandex policy; limit ad tracking in Android settings.

5. Guest mode (no account)

No server account exists. To remove local data: uninstall Expansion or clear app data in Android settings.

6. Partial deletion without deleting the account

There is no option to delete only email or only progress while keeping the account. Only full account deletion (sections 1–2) or clearing local guest data (section 5).
